Talent Enhancement

Offers comprehensive Talent Enhancement programs designed to nurture and cultivate the unique skills and potential of every young individual in our community.

Welcome to Talent Enhancement Workshop of CYDO Santa Rosa

Talent Enhancement Showcase

Every twice a year, the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) organizes a Talent Enhancement Workshop Showcase, highlighting the diverse talents of the city's youth. This event features performances in singing, dancing, guitar, and keyboard, providing a platform for young individuals to showcase their skills and creativity. The workshop not only celebrates the artistic abilities of Santa Rosa's youth but also encourages their personal growth and development through the arts.

Singing Workshop

The Singing Workshop organized by the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) provides expert training in vocal techniques and performance skills for young singers. Participants receive personalized coaching and engage in interactive sessions to develop confidence and proficiency in singing, promoting musical growth and artistic expression.

Discover the power of your voice in our Singing Workshop, where you'll learn vocal techniques and performance skills in a supportive environment. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned singer, this is your chance to shine and connect with fellow music enthusiasts.

Guitar Workshop

The Guitar Workshop hosted by the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) provides specialized training in guitar techniques and musical performance. Participants benefit from personalized coaching and interactive sessions, enhancing their skills and confidence in playing the guitar.

Grab your guitar and join us for the Guitar Workshop, where you'll learn to strum, pick, and play your favorite songs. From basic chords to advanced techniques, this workshop is perfect for anyone looking to improve their guitar skills and jam with others.

Dance Workshop

The Dance Workshop organized by the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) offers specialized training in dance techniques and performance. Participants engage in personalized coaching sessions and interactive classes, enhancing their skills and confidence in various dance styles.

Get ready to move and groove in our Dance Workshop, where you'll learn fun routines and express yourself through dance. Whether you’re into hip-hop, contemporary, or any other style, this workshop offers a dynamic space to explore your passion for dance.

Keyboard Workshop

The Keyboard Workshop conducted by the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) focuses on developing proficiency in keyboard playing and musical expression. Through hands-on training and personalized instruction, participants learn essential techniques and explore diverse musical genres.

Unlock your musical potential in the Keyboard Workshop, where you'll dive into the world of melodies and harmonies. With guidance from skilled instructors, you’ll learn to play the keyboard and create beautiful music in a collaborative setting.

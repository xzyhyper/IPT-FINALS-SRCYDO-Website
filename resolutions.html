Resolutions

To set forth strategic goals and action plans aimed at enhancing youth engagement and development within our community.

Welcome to Resolution Section of the CYDO Santa Rosa

The Resolution Section of the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) is vital for creating rules that benefit all young people in the city. The CYDO focuses on making resolutions that support city-wide youth goals, ensuring comprehensive development and engagement. These resolutions address areas such as community involvement, leadership, education, and recreation, aiming to meet the diverse needs of all youth in Santa Rosa City.

Similarly, the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) plays a crucial role in this section. The SK creates resolutions that support the goals of youth in the barangays, following the Comprehensive Barangay Youth Development Plan and the Sangguniang Kabataan Reform Act. By addressing local needs and initiatives, the SK ensures that the specific needs of barangay youth are met. Together, the CYDO and SK work to ensure that the needs of all young people in Santa Rosa City are comprehensively addressed and supported.

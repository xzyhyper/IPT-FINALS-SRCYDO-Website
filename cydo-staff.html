IN SERVICE FOR THE YOUTH

Welcome to Santa Rosa City Youth Development Staff

The Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (SRYDO) operates with professionalism across its team. Led by a dedicated Youth Development Officer, the CYDO head oversees strategic planning and program implementation. Roles such as the Social Media Manager ensure effective online engagement, while the Administrative Head maintains smooth daily operations.

The Youth Council Relation Officer fosters collaboration with youth councils, and the SK Relation Officer ensures alignment with Sangguniang Kabataan initiatives. The Photo Documentator captures and highlights the impact of youth programs visually. Together, they aim to empower Santa Rosa City's youth through coordinated efforts, promoting growth and participation in the community.

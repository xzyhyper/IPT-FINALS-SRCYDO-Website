Youth Organization Registration Program

Welcome to Youth Serving Organization of Santa Rosa CYDO

Youth-serving organizations in Santa Rosa City, recognized through the Youth Organization Registration Program (YORP) and supported by the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (SRYDO), focus on providing essential services and support to young people, addressing their diverse needs and challenges. These organizations collaborate with schools, communities, and families to offer programs related to education, health, career development, and social services. Their goal is to create a holistic support system that promotes the well-being, safety, and development of youth. Examples include after-school programs, mentorship initiatives, and non-profits dedicated to youth advocacy and support.

Awards

To recognize and celebrate the exceptional achievements and contributions of our city's youth in various fields.

Welcome to Awards Section of the CYDO Santa Rosa

The Awards Section of the Santa Rosa City Youth Development Office (CYDO) recognizes both the outstanding achievements of the CYDO as a whole and the exceptional contributions of its individual members. This section celebrates a wide range of accomplishments, from successful youth programs and initiatives spearheaded by the office to the personal milestones and leadership demonstrated by CYDO members. By highlighting these achievements, the Awards Section not only acknowledges the hard work and dedication of the entire office but also inspires continued excellence and commitment among the youth and staff involved in Santa Rosa City's development efforts.

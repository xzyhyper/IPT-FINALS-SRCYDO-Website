Youth Organization Registration Program

WHAT IS YORP?

Youth Organization Registration Program (YORP) is a program of the National Youth Commission (NYC). This is to ensure access and participation to a City Wide initiated program within Santa Rosa City. Through this program, the office can link to the youth to provide meaningful programs.

Step by Step To be YORP Registered

To be YORP registered, follow these steps:

1. First, download the templates provided below.
2. Update the templates with the specific information of your organization.
3. Register your organization on the YORP website.
4. Wait for the confirmation email.
5. Ensure that you meet all the requirements.
6. After meeting the requirements, wait a few days for processing.
7. We will email you once your application is accepted.

Benefits of YORP

Youth Organization Registration Program (YORP) is a program of the National Youth Commission (NYC). This is to ensure access and participation to a City Wide initiated program within Santa Rosa City. Through this program the office can link to the youth to provide meaningful programs.

GOALS OF YORP

1. To inform the youth about CYDO / NYC Programs and Projects.
2. To encourage youth participation in CYDO / NYC programs (CYDO / NYC initiates activities and implements with youth).
3. To establish a communication network between youth and other youth development stakeholders.
4. To establish a youth and youth-serving organizations’ directory.
5. To disseminate updates on youth-related programs, projects and activities.
6. To provide a structure that links youth and youth-serving organizations together.
